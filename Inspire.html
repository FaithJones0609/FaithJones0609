What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Bob Ross was an American painter and art instructor who created and hosted The Joy of Painting, an instructional television program.His quiet voice narrated the actions on his canvas while simultaneously weaving little philosophies and feel-good sayings into the show.
